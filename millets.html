Millet Upma

Millet Upma is a healthy and nutritious South Indian breakfast made with millet grains, fresh vegetables, and aromatic spices. Rich in fiber, protein, vitamins, and minerals, millet upma is a wholesome alternative to traditional rava upma. It supports digestion, helps maintain energy levels, and is suitable for a balanced diet.The combination of vegetables and millet creates a flavorful and satisfying meal that can be enjoyed for breakfast, lunch, or dinner.
